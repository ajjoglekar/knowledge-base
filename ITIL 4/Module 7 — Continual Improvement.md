---

> [!context] **Scenario**  
> NextGen experiences recurring service slowdowns during peak usage.

---

> [!now] **Where Are We Now?**  
> - Incident metrics show fast recovery  
> - Customer satisfaction is declining

---

> [!vision] **What Is the Vision?**  
> - Fewer repeat incidents  
> - Predictable performance under load

---

> [!target] **Where Do We Want to Be?**  
> - Fewer performance-related incidents  
> - Earlier detection of degradation  
> - Clear ownership of improvement actions

---

> [!plan] **How Do We Get There?**  
> - Improve monitoring thresholds  
> - Refine capacity assumptions  
> - Adjust release readiness criteria  

Decisions are based on **evidence**, not blame.

---

> [!action] **Take Action**  
> - Monitoring is enhanced  
> - Load testing is updated  
> - Teams share lessons learned

---

> [!measure] **Did We Get There?**  
> - Incident frequency drops  
> - Customer complaints reduce  
> - Team stress decreases  

Improvement success is **verified**, not assumed.

---

> [!momentum] **How Do We Keep Momentum?**  
> - Improvements are logged and reviewed  
> - Metrics remain visible  
> - Learning becomes routine  

Improvement becomes **part of daily work**, not an exception.

---

> [!summary] **System-Level Insight**  
> Continual Improvement prevented NextGen from mistaking  
> **fast recovery for real progress**.

## ðŸ“Œ What Are Guiding Principles? 
**Guiding Principles** are **universal recommendations** that guide **decision-making and behavior** in **all circumstances**.

They are intentionally designed to be:

- **High-level**  
  They do not prescribe steps or procedures.  
  Instead, they influence *judgment* when choosing between options.

- **Context-agnostic**  
  They apply regardless of:
  - Team structure
  - Technology stack
  - Service type
  - Organizational maturity

- **Always relevant**  
  While tools, processes, and architectures change,  
  good decision-making principles remain valid.

Guiding Principles help answer a fundamental question:

> **â€œGiven uncertainty, what is the right thing to do?â€**
